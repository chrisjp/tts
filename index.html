Text-to-Speech Demo

A simple web app demonstrating how text sounds in different TTS voices.

Choose from any of 300+ voices from Amazon Polly (via Streamlabs API), IBM Watson, Google Translate, iSpeech, and Oddcast.
Polly voices can be useful for testing how Twitch.tv donations will sound as many streamers make use of these.
